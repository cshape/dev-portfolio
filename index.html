Support Article Feedback Collector

A custom-built tool to collect user feedback on support articles.

The Clio Help Center that I manage receives more than 5000 unique hits per day and is a significant resource for our customers. The site (powered by Zendesk) gives users the ability to upvote or downvote articles.

I wanted to see why people were downvoting, but Zendesk doesn't have that feature. So I created a custom tool which prompts users for feedback and sends their responses to my Node.js/MongoDB backend. This has been a handy way to see which articles need updating or adjusting, and also is a source of data for our customer journey team.

Technologies used:

  • CSS
  • jQuery
  • Node.js
  • Express
  • MongoDB
